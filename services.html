MOVING DETAILS

On the date previously agreed, our team will be ready to pack, transport, and assemble all your items at the new destination.

For all local house moves in the Greater Bristol area, we offer a range of free removal boxes*.

Wardrobe boxes with hanging rails are also available at no extra charge on your moving day upon arrangement.


*All standard boxes are free. However, a £2 deposit per box is due on delivery. The value is refundable after collection or return.


Furniture assembly/disassembly, and carpet protections are available upon request.


Chiaveli Removals offers a comprehensive packing service for all our customers in Bristol and surrounding areas.

We are committed to sustainability by either reusing or recycling packing materials. To do so, it’s possible to schedule a day to pick up all the packages without any extra costs.
Rest assured that all our services include insurance of the transported objects and the surrounding premises.
